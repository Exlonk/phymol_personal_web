My name is Exlonk Gil, I am a graduate of the Faculty of Exact and Natural Sciences at Antioquia University. I am currently pursuing a PhD in the Nicoulaus Copernicus University in Toruń-Poland under the PHYMOL doctoral network , focusing on a project entitled 'Quantum Chemistry and Machine Learning applied to predicting fluorescence quantum yield'. This project explores the integration of quantum mechanics and machine learning predict fluorescence quantum yield in organic molecules by leveraging quantum mechanical calculations and advanced machine learning algorithms. The study aims to enhance the accuracy of predictions related to photophysical properties. This approach could streamline the design of new fluorescent materials by providing rapid and reliable predictions My academic and professional journey has endowed me with a profound knowledge and skill set in data science, particularly in Python programming.
